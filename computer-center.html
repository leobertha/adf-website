ADF Computer Center — The Bakeli Partnership

  • Conakry, Guinea, West Africa

In partnership with Volkeno Senegal, our Bakeli program aims to train 4,000 African youths in web and software development skills over the next four years. This ambitious goal would place at least 50% of our students within 6 months of graduation from the program. A virtual internship program will allow students in our programs (in the US and across Africa) to learn and grow with partner organizations across the globe via virtual internships.
